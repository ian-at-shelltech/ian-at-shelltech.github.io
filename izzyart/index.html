Izzy Sempill
Izzy's Art Multidisciplinary artist - Art teacher - Community practitioner

Hi, I'm Izzy

I'm a multidisciplinary artist and art teacher, based in West Yorkshire.

As an artist, my work spans a range of mediums. I find so much joy in experimentation and learning new techniques - exploring the textures, properties and possibilities of different materials.

I tend not to plan my work too carefully. I prefer to let the materials guide me and embrace unexpected outcomes, then refine the process until I'm satisfied with the result.

On this site, you'll find prints of some of my collages, life drawings and mixed media pieces. There are original pieces for sale too - I've been really enjoying working with texture paste and watercolour on wooden boards lately!

I have loads of experience with schools and young people. I worked in various pastoral roles with secondary and college students before training to become an art teacher. I'm a strong advocate for arts education and ensuring young people have access to creative and cultural opportunities.

I am currently broadening my practice to engage with local communities - something I have always wanted to do - through studying an MA in Socially Engaged Arts Practice.
