Postcard Art

Water Street Gallery, Todmorden

I'm really pleased to have my work exhibited at Water Street Gallery. I created two emulsion transfers of wintery scenes for their Postcard Art call-out. I love postcard-sized art because it's accessible, affordable and welcomes a broad range of styles and mediums.

Emulsion transfers can have an ethereal, worn quality to them. For me, they show how photographs can represent faded memories. I think transfers work well as postcard-sized pieces - when the viewer looks closely, they'll start to notice the layers, marks and textures that are created naturally through the process.

As with film photography, the outcome of an emulsion transfer is unclear until the very end, which helps me to relinquish control of the outcome and enjoy the making process!

My work is displayed alongside larger pieces from the Seeds of Change collection. The exhibitions run from Nov 2024 - Jan 2025.
